About LifeSaver

Our Mission

Founded in 2025, LifeSaver connects blood donors with recipients through an efficient, transparent platform. We've facilitated over many successful donations across cities.

Our Team

We're a team of healthcare professionals, developers, and community volunteers committed to saving lives through technology.

Location Tracking

Our platform uses secure geolocation to:

  • Find the nearest donation centers
  • Alert donors when someone nearby needs blood
  • Optimize blood distribution
